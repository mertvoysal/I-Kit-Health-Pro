About the I-Kit Project

I-Kit is an AI-based digital health assistant developed to guide family physicians and patients in the early diagnosis of thyroid diseases.

  • TUBITAK (Scientific and Technological Research Council of Turkey) - 2209-A Program
  • Advisor: Prof. Dr. Gözde Ulutagay
  • Manager: Mert Voysal | Researcher: Caner Doğukan Aydemir
  • Engine: Trained using the CatBoost model.

Dataset scope note: this prototype is trained on the Garvan thyroid dataset, one of the most widely used educational benchmark datasets in thyroid ML studies. Its panel is strong for decision-support training but mainly includes TSH, T3, TT4, T4U, and FTI rather than a full modern hormone panel.

Clinical responsibility belongs to licensed healthcare professionals; this tool is for decision support only.
